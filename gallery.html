Pet Photo Gallery

This is Mowgli, our Australian Shepherd. He grew up on a farm surrounded by cats, and we suspect he thinks he is a cat given some of his eccentricities. Doesn't like strangers, but really he's a big sweetheart.
This is Misha, our oldest cat and a representative of the previous cat administration. She's getting a bit chubby in her later years, but she still enjoys roaming around the property and trying to hunt.
This is Heera, a pure Maine Coon and mother to our other 3 cats. We got her from a family in North York who were rehoming their cat, and didn't know that she was pregnant. Great value cat!

This is Walter, who inherited an all-white coat and blue eyes, presumably from his father. Apparently white cats with blue eyes are typically deaf, but Walter can hear just fine. Loves a good cuddle.
This is Roxie, who was the adorable runt of her litter. She's much smaller than her brothers and mother, with shorter legs and body but plenty of fluff. Very independent, but also sweet and playful.
