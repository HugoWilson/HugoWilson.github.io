Where I'm From

I was born and raised in Port Perry, Ontario. (See the upper map on the right!)

My Educational Background

Growing up, I was always drawn to the natural spaces around my town, especially the Durham Regional Forest where I volunteered during the summer.

Ultimately, my desire to spend more of my time in nature is what drew me to study Environmental Technology at Fleming College.

During my first stint at Fleming, I found that I was especially proficient in certain software elements of the program, including GIS.

I even ended up using my GIS education while working a student job with York Region's Transportation Department.

A couple years after graduating, I decided to return to school and take a post-graduate course to further my GIS knowledge.

I'm currently enrolled in the Fleming College GIS Applications Specialist program.
